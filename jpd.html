About Me

I am an ex-teacher of a highly reputed private school in Kolkata and have been successfully teaching mathematics and physics to students of class VII-XII for the last 22 years. I have graduated with hons from St. Xaviers' College, Kolkata and done my B. Tech from JU.I handle IGCSE, GCSE, IB, CBSE, ICSE and other boards. Presently I have students from most of the major cities in India and abroad specially Great Britain, America, Australia, Malaysia, Oman, Sharjah, Dubai, Qatar and some other countries. I believe students are like a lump of clay, it depends on the sculpture to give them a perfect shape. A good teacher should be a source of inspiration to his students to strive for success. That's what I try to do.
